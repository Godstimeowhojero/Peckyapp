Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-20

This Privacy Policy explains what information Pecky ("Pecky," "we," "us," or "our") collects when you use the Pecky mobile application (the "App"), why we collect it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

If you have questions, contact us at support@peckyapp.com.

Quick summary — not a substitute for the full policy

  • We collect only what's needed to run your reminders and sync them across devices: your email, a display name, the tasks/notes you write, your daily completion history, your preferences, and your subscription status.
  • No ads, no analytics, no advertising trackers — and we never sell or rent your data.
  • Your data lives in a Supabase-hosted database (for cloud sync) and locally on your device. Payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google.
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data at any time, whether or not you still have the App installed.

01Information We Collect

Account information

When you create a Pecky account, we — through our authentication provider, Supabase — collect your email address and password (stored by Supabase in hashed form; we never see or store your plaintext password).

Profile and app settings

To personalize reminders and keep your setup in sync across devices, we store: display name, language preference, daily task goal, quiet hours, streak warning time, notification style ("Pecky intensity"), light/dark theme preference, escalating-reminders on/off setting, onboarding completion status, and current/best streak counts.

Task content

Everything you enter to track your tasks and habits: task titles and notes, due dates and repeat rules, activity type (simple task, tally, timed, or measured) with any target/progress amounts or units, and completion timestamps.

Please don't enter sensitive information — medical details, financial account numbers, or other people's personal information — into task titles or notes. Pecky treats this content like any other text you type, and it's stored and synced the same way as the rest of your task data.

Daily activity history

For each day you use Pecky, we store how many tasks you completed and whether you met your daily goal that day. This powers your activity calendar and streak tracking and is synced across your devices the same way as your other account data.

Subscription and purchase information

Pecky Pro is billed and processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play — we never see or store your payment card details. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlements: RevenueCat receives your purchase/transaction data from Apple or Google and reports your subscription status back to us, linked to your Pecky account ID.

Device and local data

Pecky keeps a local copy of your tasks, settings, and streak data on your device so the App works offline, and schedules reminder notifications directly on your device. This local database also keeps a small operational log of which in-app messages (e.g., completion quotes) you've already been shown, purely so Pecky doesn't repeat itself — this log never leaves your device. Your sign-in session is stored in your device's secure credential storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

What we do not collect

Pecky does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs; does not request access to your camera, photo library, contacts, or location; and does not display third-party ads. We do not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

02How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to create and maintain your account and sync your data across devices, schedule and deliver the reminders and escalation ladder you've configured, track your streaks, daily goal progress, and activity history, verify your Pecky Pro subscription status, respond to support requests, and keep the App secure and working correctly.

We do not use your task content, notes, or account information to train AI/ML models, and we do not share it with data brokers or advertisers.

03Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

  • Contract — to provide the App and the features you've signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the App secure and working correctly
  • Consent — where required, e.g., for notification permissions
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose information to comply with law

05Data Retention

We retain your account and task data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time directly in the App (Settings → Delete account), which immediately and permanently deletes your account and all associated data — tasks, settings, activity history, and streak history — from our production systems, except where we're required to retain certain records for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes. Local data on your device is removed when you uninstall the App.

Deleting your account does not cancel an active App Store or Google Play subscription. Cancel that separately in your device's subscription settings if you want to stop future charges.

06Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS) between the App and our servers, encrypted on-device storage of your sign-in credentials, and access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security.

07Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, correct inaccurate information, delete your account and all associated data, receive a copy of your data in a portable format, object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

In the App: delete your account directly in Settings → Delete account — this takes effect immediately.

Without the App installed: if you no longer have Pecky installed, or want to exercise any other right above, email us at support@peckyapp.com from the address associated with your account. We'll verify your identity and complete deletion or access requests within the timeframe required by applicable law (e.g., 30 days under GDPR, 45 days under CCPA/CPRA), and in any case no later than 45 days.

California residents: you have the right to know, delete, and correct your personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not collect "sensitive personal information" as defined by the CPRA. We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as a valid opt-out request, though as noted above we don't sell or share data regardless of any signal.

08International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process and store data in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, for these transfers.

09Children's Privacy

Pecky is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in these age groups. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@peckyapp.com and we will delete it.

10Notifications

Pecky only sends local, on-device notifications generated by the App itself based on your reminders and settings. We do not use remote/push notification services, and we do not collect a push token or any data about the notifications you receive.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you in the App or by email before the change takes effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
